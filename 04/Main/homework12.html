Almaty

Introduction to the City:

Almaty is the largest city in Kazakhstan, known for its beautiful landscapes and vibrant culture.

Geography

Almaty is located in the southeastern part of Kazakhstan, surrounded by mountains

Culture

Almaty has a rich cultural heritage, with a mix of traditional Kazakh culture and modern lifestyle.

Economy

Almaty is a major economic hub in Kazakhstan, with a diverse economy that includes finance, trade, and tourism.
