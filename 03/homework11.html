The Nauryz

A traditional celebration marking the beginning of spring.

Key Features of Nauryz:

  • Celebrated on March 21st, the vernal equinox.
  • Symbolizes renewal, rebirth, and the start of a new year.
  • Involves various customs such as cleaning homes, preparing special meals, and wearing traditional clothing.
  • People often gather with family and friends to share meals and celebrate together.
